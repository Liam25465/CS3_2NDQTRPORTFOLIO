Shouei Barou

  • A wildcard in the lineup of the Ubers, Barou Shouei serves as the main striker of the defensive based club.
  • With his unpredictable dribbling and relentless attacks, he can cut through other team's defenses with ease.

    • Rin Itoshi

  • Rin Itoshi, the rising star emerging from Blue Lock, the little brother of the well-known Sae Itoshi.
  • Just like his brother, Rin dominates the field with his raw talent that lies within his dribbles and curve shots
    • making him a force to be reckoned with. Whenever playing against him, always get ready to put your LIFE ON THE LINE.

    Ryusei Shidou

  • Making an EXPLOSIVE entrance into the field, Shidou Ryusei is a striker no one has ever seen before. With his
  • unpredictable movements around the field, he leaves everybody dazed just trying to anticipate his next move.
  • The way he can strike the ball into the goal with unpredictable moves are what sets him apart from normal strikers.

    • Seishiro Nagi

  • Nagi is a young prodigy that is a master at controlling and trapping the ball. At instances
  • Where average joes would think that the ball is beyond saving, Nagi will prove them wrong
  • for his surprising touches and intercepts on the ball says otherwise.

    • Hyoma Chigiri

  • The pink panther, Hyoma Chigri, can blitz through the field with ease. Gifted with great legs for running,
  • he brings the ball to distances far from the enemy's reach. He can turn the tides easily as he wishes, however,
  • with the acl injury on his right knee from his younger years, he is a ticking time bomb that can explode at any moment.
  • He continues to play every game like it is his last, as once he explodes, he can never return to soccer ever again.

    • Meguru Bachira

  • Float like a butterfly, and sting like a bee. Bachira's unreal dribbling is known to be one of the best,
  • if not the best, in Blue Lock. Once with the ball, he moves gracefully with it, as if dancing, and dribbles past
  • other players with ease. Dribbling however is not just his strong attribute as his passes and shooting are to be
  • noticed as well. The monster in him is no longer there for he IS the monster.

    • Yoichi Isagi

  • The Genius of Adaptability, The Demon King, The All-Seeing. With many titles to his name, Isagi lives up to
  • what they say he is. His dribbling may not be the best, nor is his shooting, but his ability to see everything
  • in the field is what makes him Blue Lock's trump card. Any play that is happening in the field, he will find a way
  • to counter it, or amplify its effectiveness. He doesn't score through brute force, but intuition, analyzation, and
  • anticipation. Wherever the ball goes, he will be there, ALWAYS. Everyone else are playing checkers, he however, is playing
  • chess.

    • Micheal Kaiser

  • The German superstar who rose from the slums, Kaiser, is known to have the fastest shots ever in the Neo Egoist League.
  • He can generate powerful shots with such a short wind-up. His dribbling is not something to be ignored either,
  • the paths he takes to slip past enemies with his off-ball movement is something to be given praise of. Unlike
  • other players, interceptions and defense don't work on him effectively, even if he is knocked down, or ragdolled,
  • he will find a way to shoot that ball with that same terrifying power as when on ground.
